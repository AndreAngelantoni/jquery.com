Known Issues

The following browsers have problems:

  • Mozilla Firefox 1.0.x
  • Internet Explorer 1.0-5.x
  • Safari 1.0-2.0.1
  • Opera 1.0-9.x
  • Konqueror
jQuery generally works with Konqueror and Firefox 1.0.x, but there may be some unexpected bugs since we do not test them regularly.
